# Neon Snake Arena - Background Music

## Track List

This folder contains 5 background music tracks for Neon Snake Arena.

### Track 1: song1.mp3 - Menu/Main Theme
- **Tempo**: 90-100 BPM
- **Mood**: Nostalgic, inviting, atmospheric
- **Use**: Title screen, menu navigation
- **Style**: Dreamy synthwave with arpeggiated pads
- **Key**: C Major or F Major

### Track 2: song2.mp3 - Early Gameplay (Levels 1-3)
- **Tempo**: 115-125 BPM
- **Mood**: Focused, building energy
- **Use**: Regular gameplay, early levels
- **Style**: Light chiptune with synthwave bass
- **Key**: A Minor or E Minor

### Track 3: song3.mp3 - Mid Gameplay (Levels 4-5)
- **Tempo**: 125-135 BPM
- **Mood**: Intensifying, more urgent
- **Use**: Later regular levels, increasing difficulty
- **Style**: Full chipwave fusion
- **Key**: Same as Track 2 for seamless transition

### Track 4: song4.mp3 - Boss/Intense (Level 6+)
- **Tempo**: 140-150 BPM
- **Mood**: Intense, urgent, high stakes
- **Use**: Boss battles (Level 6), hazard levels (7-9)
- **Style**: Dark synth, industrial elements
- **Key**: D Minor or G Minor (darker)

### Track 5: song5.mp3 - Bonus Level (Level 10)
- **Tempo**: 130-140 BPM
- **Mood**: Celebratory, exciting, rewarding
- **Use**: Level 10 Food Frenzy bonus
- **Style**: Upbeat synthwave with chiptune flourishes
- **Key**: E Major or A Major (major shift for reward feeling)

## Technical Requirements

- **Format**: MP3 (192 kbps recommended) or Opus (fallback)
- **Looping**: Tracks should be designed to loop seamlessly
- **Duration**: 2-3 minutes each (will loop during gameplay)
- **Volume**: Normalized to -24 LUFS for consistent levels

## In-Game Controls

Press **M** to cycle through music tracks:
- Track 0: Silent Mode
- Tracks 1-5: Background music

## Music System Features

- Automatic track looping
- Crossfade between tracks
- Volume balancing with sound effects
- Per-track display in ready screen
